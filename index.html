About CosmicCoin

CosmicCoin (COS) is revolutionizing the cryptocurrency space by providing a secure, fast, and scalable blockchain solution for the future of finance. Built on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC), COS offers a deflationary tokenomics model, community-driven growth, and a robust ecosystem for decentralized applications.

Our Mission

To create a decentralized ecosystem that empowers individuals and businesses to take control of their financial future, fostering innovation, security, and prosperity through:

  • Community-driven decision-making
  • Transparent and secure transactions
  • Scalable and efficient blockchain technology
  • Education and adoption initiatives
  • Strategic partnerships and collaborations

Our Vision

To become a leading cryptocurrency platform, recognized for its:

  • Decentralized governance
  • Advanced security measures
  • High-performance scalability
  • Inclusive community engagement
  • Sustainable ecosystem growth

Vesting Schedule

  • Airdrop: 20% at TGE, then 20% quarterly over 1 year
  • Liquidity: 100% at TGE for immediate market availability
  • Team: 12-month cliff, then 25% quarterly over 1 year
  • Treasury: 25% at TGE, then 25% quarterly over 9 months
  • Investors: 25% at TGE, then 25% monthly over 3 months
  • Advisors: 6-month cliff, then 25% quarterly over 1 year
  • Reserved: At the discretion of the governance process

Token Utility

  • Governance: COS holders can participate in decision-making processes
  • Staking: Earn rewards by locking up COS tokens
  • Transaction fees: Reduced fees for COS holders on our platform
  • Access to premium features: Unlock exclusive content and services

Fee Structure

A transaction fee is applied to buy and sell transactions, with a portion redistributed to holders and added to the liquidity pool.
